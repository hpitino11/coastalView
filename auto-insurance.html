Common Auto Insurance Questions

Answers to what we hear most often.

Florida requires at minimum $10,000 in Personal Injury Protection (PIP) and $10,000 in Property Damage Liability (PDL). However, these minimums often aren't enough to fully protect you; we'll help you find the right level of coverage for your situation.

PIP covers a portion of medical expenses and lost wages for you and passengers in your vehicle, regardless of who caused the accident. Florida is a no-fault state, which is why PIP is required. It applies whether you were at fault or not.
